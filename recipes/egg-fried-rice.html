Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 14 oz Uncooked jasmine rice
  • 14 oz Water
  • 1 oz Green onion
  • 4 Large eggs
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Dark soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Light soy sauce

Steps

  1. Wash and cook rice
  2. Cut green onions and carrot
  3. Crack and season eggs
