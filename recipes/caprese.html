Caprese Salad

Caprese salad is yummy yummy nom nom

Ingredients

  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Slice tomatoes and mozzarella and layer them onto a platter with fresh basil leaves
  2. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with salt and pepper
  3. Serve!
